## Typography

### Font Family:
- **Manrope**  
  Use **Manrope** across all UI elements for readability and consistency.

### Font Weights:
- **Light (300):** Used for subtle elements, placeholders, and secondary text.
- **Regular (400):** Default for body text and general content.
- **Medium (500):** For medium emphasis.
- **Semi-Bold (600):** For section headers, labels, and highlighted content.
- **Bold (700):** For titles and headings.

### Font Sizes:
- **Heading 1 (H1):** 24px
- **Heading 2 (H2):** 20px
- **Heading 3 (H3):** 16px
- **Body Text:** 14px (minimum)
- **Caption Text:** 13px
